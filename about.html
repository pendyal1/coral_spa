About Us

Wellness, thoughtfully practiced for Kanpur.

Coral Spa is a calm city retreat created by a devoted wellness team with more than a decade of experience in spa therapies, grooming and holistic care.

Mission

To make restorative wellness accessible without compromising calm, hygiene or care.

Coral Spa brings together authentic spa wisdom and contemporary therapies so every guest can experience physical relaxation, mental ease and a more balanced state of wellbeing.

The spa is minimalist yet luxurious, reflecting the elements of nature while keeping the treatment journey focused, grounded and personal.

History

Built by continuous practice.

From its earlier city presence to the current Vishnu Puri location, Coral Spa has continued to evolve around the same idea: a reliable wellness getaway in your own city.

Over a decade in wellness

The Coral Spa team has worked in spa and beauty care for more than ten years, building a menu that balances relaxation with measurable therapeutic value.

Heart of Kanpur

The spa welcomes guests at Basement, B.K-2 Tower 3/13A, Vishnu Puri, Company Bagh Chauraha, Kanpur-208002.

Nature-led design

The space is created to feel simple, warm and grounded, with treatments that help guests step away from the pace of the city.

The team

Trained therapists and aestheticians.

Every therapy is guided by a trained professional who considers technique, pressure, skin type, comfort and the outcome the guest wants from the visit.

Therapists

Experienced in Swedish, Thai oil, Thai dry, Balinese, deep tissue, hot stone, potli, foot and stress-relief therapies.

Aestheticians

Focused on facials, clean-ups, de-tanning, body polish and body wraps with product selection matched to the guest's needs.

Beauty team

Available for bookings and guidance across manicures, pedicures, hair spa and treatment combinations.

Facilities

Designed for comfort from arrival to afterglow.

  • An enchanting spa reception lounge.
  • Six spacious therapy suites with low-rise beds and attached steam and shower cubicles.
  • One facial and body polish room with a high-rise bed and attached steam and shower cubicle.
  • A hair spa section with a hair treatment chair and shampoo station.
  • Two manicure and pedicure recliners for focused hand and foot care.
